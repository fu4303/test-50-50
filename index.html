The Quest

There’s a scene in the movie, Ratatouille, where Anton Ego, the critical food critic, takes a bite of the titular dish. He is instantly transported to his childhood. There is something about being in that moment. It’s a moment I seek. The rest of world disappears. I can no longer hear. I can no longer see. All I can do is taste. And it is pure pleasure.

This journey will take me around the world, to visit 50 of the World’s Best Restaurants. I am no food critic. I have no sway. I’m just a guy who enjoys really good food and enjoys to travel.

I’ve put together some resources to help along the way. I have a list of all the restaurants. Each year, a new list will come out and I will update this spreadsheet.

I’ve also put together maps showing where in the world each restaurant is. There is the 2017 Map and 2018 Map

What you’ll find here is a history of all the places I’ve been.

Enjoy!

The Quest

In 2018, shortly after my 44th birthday, I set myself a goal to visit 50 of the World's 50 Best Restaurants before I turn 50.

A new list comes out every year, with new restaurants added to the list and some restaurants falling off the list. I can visit any restaurant that was on the list between 2017 and 2022.

Why?

I love to travel and I love great food. The list provides an excuse to travel to new places around the world all while enjoying some of the best food on the planet.
