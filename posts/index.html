The Quest

In 2018, shortly after my 44th birthday, I set myself a goal to visit 50 of the World's 50 Best Restaurants before I turn 50.

A new list comes out every year, with new restaurants added to the list and some restaurants falling off the list. I can visit any restaurant that was on the list between 2017 and 2022.

Why?

I love to travel and I love great food. The list provides an excuse to travel to new places around the world all while enjoying some of the best food on the planet.
